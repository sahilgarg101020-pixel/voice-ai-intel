## Where the puck is going

**STT's next frontier isn't accuracy — it's awareness.** AssemblyAI's Context Carryover addresses a problem hiding in plain sight: your STT model has been transcribing the caller without knowing what your agent just asked. When the agent says "Can I have your date of birth?" and the user replies "oh-three-twenty-six," a context-blind model guesses. A context-aware model knows it's hearing a date. The 26% → 9% error rate on critical utterances isn't a marginal WER improvement — it's a category shift from "transcribing audio" to "understanding the conversation." This connects directly to the June 12 clinic operator signal: turn-level context (what was just said, by whom, expecting what) matters more than aggregate WER for CSAT. Expect Deepgram, ElevenLabs Scribe, and Smallest AI Pulse to follow — context-as-input will be table stakes for agent STT within two release cycles.

**Voice AI's selling motion is shifting from developer-first to CX-operator-first.** CCW 2026 — the world's largest contact center event — was the venue for more voice AI launches this week than any developer conference. RingCentral's AIR Pro Studio and Amazon Connect's Agentic CX Designer both ship no-code visual builders for voice agents. The pattern: the infrastructure is mature enough that the bottleneck is no longer "can we build it" but "can the business team configure, test, and ship it without engineering." This is exactly the transition that happened in web development (WordPress), email marketing (Mailchimp), and chatbots (Intercom) — the tool moves from the builder to the operator. For voice AI startups still selling to engineering teams: the buyer is migrating upstairs.

The TELUS Digital/ElevenLabs partnership crystallizes this. A global systems integrator committing to implement and govern a voice AI startup's platform across five enterprise CX stacks (Genesys, Twilio, Connect, Zendesk, Salesforce) is enterprise adoption infrastructure, not a tech demo. It's the "big four consulting firm picks a platform" moment for voice AI.
