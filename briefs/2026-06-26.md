## Where the puck is going

**Healthcare is voice AI's first fully funded vertical.** Prosper AI's $30M from a16z — on top of Deepgram presenting healthcare deployment patterns at HLTH Europe last week and Microsoft Copilot Studio voice agents going GA for Dynamics 365 Contact Center — marks the moment healthcare stops being a "use case" and becomes a market category. The dynamics are clear: healthcare runs on phone calls (scheduling, prior auth, billing), the calls are structured and high-volume, compliance requirements (HIPAA) create switching costs, and the ROI is measurable (Prosper claims 80% win rate in competitive evals). For builders: healthcare is the template for how voice AI verticalizes — high call volume, structured workflows, compliance moat, measurable outcomes. Insurance and financial services are next.

**The no-code voice agent builder is now table stakes.** CCW 2026 ended June 25, and the final tally is striking: Zoom (Agent Architect), Amazon Connect (Agentic CX Designer), RingCentral (AIR Pro Studio), Talkdesk (Agent Builder), Salesforce (Agentforce WEM), Dialpad, and 8x8 all now ship prompt-to-production or visual no-code voice agent builders. Every major CX platform has one. The differentiation has shifted from "do you have voice AI" to "how fast can a non-engineer ship it, and how do you test it before it talks to a customer." Zoom and Talkdesk both launched pre-deployment simulation in the same week — testing infrastructure is no longer a startup play (Coval, $28M last week), it's a platform feature. The CXToday post-mortem headline captures it: "the industry's AI honeymoon is well and truly over." What follows the honeymoon is operations discipline.

## Watchlist / open threads

- **Healthcare voice AI competitive dynamics:** Prosper AI ($30M, a16z) vs. existing players in healthcare voice (Nuance/Microsoft, Amazon Connect health verticals). Watch for Bland AI (healthcare focus in $50M raise) and ElevenLabs (HIPAA/SOC2) to contest the vertical.
- **No-code builder convergence:** With every CX platform shipping voice agent builders, the differentiator is pre-deployment simulation quality. Watch whether platform-native testing (Zoom Performance Suite, Talkdesk validation) displaces or coexists with standalone testing platforms (Coval, Hamming).
- **Microsoft Voice Live + Foundry scope:** Voice Live bundles STT+TTS+turn detection+avatars into one API under Foundry governance. If Teams/M365 Copilot publishing goes GA this month as planned, Microsoft has end-to-end voice agent distribution — a direct platform risk for Vapi, Retell, and LiveKit in the enterprise segment.
- **Bland AI production latency (day 23):** Still no public response to field reports of P50 ~800ms.
- **AudioHijack / voice security (day 23):** Zero vendor mitigations published.
